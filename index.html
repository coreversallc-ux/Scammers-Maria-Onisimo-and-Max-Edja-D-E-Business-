After discovering this fraud, I filed formal complaints with the Small Business Administration (SBA) and B:Side to report the misconduct and begin remediation. However, the effects of their actions extended far beyond the loan itself.

Impact on Core Versatility LLC: This misconduct forced Core Versatility LLC into severe financial and operational hardship. Our tax infrastructure was destabilized, financial records were compromised, and compliance reporting became nearly impossible due to the inaccuracies created by their fraudulent filing.

In simple terms: Their deceptive actions pushed my company into financial ruin and left our tax and accounting foundation in shambles.

This behavior aligns with a broader pattern of deceitful and manipulative conduct that others should be aware of.
